Cabonara

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Parmegianno
  • egg
  • pepper
  • guiancale

Directions

  1. First fry the guiancale on medium high heat. There is no need for oil because the fat from the pork will be enough
  2. next you're going to boil the spaghetti
  3. after the spaghetti is done and the guiancale is cooked mix the noodles in the fat and meat
  4. shred parmesan and mix with raw egg, stir the sauce mixture in with the noodles and meat and voila
