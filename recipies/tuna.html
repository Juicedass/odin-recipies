Tuna steak

Back to Home

Ingredients

  • Tuna steak
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive oil

Directions

  1. first pour olive oil onto a cast iron pan or any stainless steel pan
  2. pat dry tuna steak and season liberally with salt and pepper
  3. once pan is smoking hot place seasoned tuna steaks on pan flipping about a minute and thirty seconds later
  4. once both sides have developed a brown crust they are ready to eat, dont worry if steak is very pink in the middle thats where all the flavor is
  5. mourn the majestic fish that gave its life for such a delectiable meal and proceed to eat 3 or 4 of them because theyre that good
